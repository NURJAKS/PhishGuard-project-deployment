# Privacy Policy for PhishGuard AI

**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction
PhishGuard AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the PhishGuard AI Chrome Extension.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our core security features:
- **URLs Analyzed:** When you visit a website, the extension may send the URL to our backend server (https://phishguard.ddns.net) for AI-powered phishing analysis.
- **Email Metadata:** If enabled, the extension analyzes metadata of opened emails (sender domain, links) to detect phishing attempts. We do **not** read or store the full content of your private emails.
- **Account Information:** If you create an account, we store your username and hashed password to manage your subscription plan and scan limits.

## 3. How We Use Information
The collected information is used solely for:
- Detecting and blocking phishing websites and malicious links.
- Providing security reports and analytics in your dashboard.
- Improving our AI detection models.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **never** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data is processed securely on our dedicated servers.

## 5. User Controls
- **Auto-Scan Toggle:** You can disable automatic background scanning at any time from the extension popup.
- **Cache Management:** You can clear your local scan history and cache via the extension settings.

## 6. Permissions Justification
- **activeTab / tabs:** To identify which website needs to be scanned for security.
- **webNavigation:** To protect you in real-time as you navigate between pages.
- **storage:** To securely save your login session and language preferences.
- **notifications:** To alert you immediately if a high-risk threat is detected.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at support@phishguard.ddns.net or through our GitHub repository.
